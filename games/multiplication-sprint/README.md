## Overview
Multiplication Sprint is a fast-paced math game where players answer 20 multiplication, addition, and subtraction questions as quickly as possible. All questions are displayed in a grid format, allowing players to tackle them in any order.

## Learning Objectives

### Primary Skills
- **Multiplication Fluency**: Practice multiplication tables from 2√ó2 to 12√ó12
- **Quick Recall**: Build automatic recall of basic multiplication facts
- **Mental Math**: Strengthen addition and subtraction skills with 2-3 digit numbers

### Secondary Skills
- **Time Management**: Complete tasks efficiently under time pressure
- **Strategic Thinking**: Choose optimal order for answering questions
- **Accuracy Focus**: Emphasize precision over speed (100% required for leaderboard)

## Game Mechanics

### Difficulty Levels

#### Easy
- **Multiplication**: Tables 2-6
- **Addition/Subtraction**: 2-digit numbers (10-99)
- **Target Audience**: Students just beginning multiplication

#### Normal
- **Multiplication**: Tables 2-10, includes some 7√óN and 9√óN
- **Addition/Subtraction**: 2-3 digit numbers (10-999)
- **Target Audience**: Students with basic multiplication knowledge

#### Hard
- **Multiplication**: All tables 2-12, emphasis on 7, 9, 11, 12
- **Addition/Subtraction**: 3-digit numbers (100-999)
- **Target Audience**: Advanced students seeking mastery

### Rules
1. **20 Questions Total**: 16 multiplication (80%), 4 addition/subtraction (20%)
2. **Time Limit**: 2 minutes (120 seconds)
3. **Multiple Choice**: 5 answer options per question
4. **Grid Format**: All questions visible simultaneously
5. **Any Order**: Answer questions in any sequence
6. **Leaderboard**: Only 100% accuracy scores are recorded

## How to Play

1. **Enter Name**: Provide your player name (stored locally)
2. **Select Difficulty**: Choose Easy, Normal, or Hard
3. **Read Instructions**: Review the welcome screen
4. **Start Game**: Click "Start Game" to begin the 2-minute timer
5. **Answer Questions**: Click answer buttons to select your choices
6. **Track Progress**: Monitor the progress bar at the top
7. **Submit**: Click "Submit Answers" when all questions are answered
8. **View Results**: See your score, accuracy, and time
9. **Check Leaderboard**: Compare your performance with others

## Pedagogical Approach

### Research-Based Strategies
1. **Distributed Practice**: Short, frequent sessions are more effective than cramming
2. **Retrieval Practice**: Active recall strengthens memory more than passive review
3. **Interleaved Practice**: Mixing operations (multiply, add, subtract) improves learning
4. **Immediate Feedback**: Visual confirmation helps correct misconceptions
5. **Mastery Learning**: 100% accuracy requirement encourages thoroughness

### Differentiation
- **Self-Paced**: Grid format allows students to work at their own speed
- **Multiple Entry Points**: Three difficulty levels accommodate diverse learners
- **Low Stakes**: Local-only leaderboards reduce anxiety
- **Immediate Retry**: Unlimited attempts encourage persistence
